Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HomeLink

How does HomeLink maintain my home IP address? +

HomeLink creates a secure tunnel between your device and your home network. All your internet traffic routes through your home connection, maintaining your home IP address and giving you access to local resources as if you were physically there.

Is there a data limit? +

No, HomeLink doesn't impose any data limits. Your data usage is only limited by your home internet plan. We believe in providing unrestricted access to your own internet connection.

What happens if my home internet goes down? +

If your home internet connection is interrupted, HomeLink will notify you immediately. We also offer an optional failover service that can temporarily route your connection through our secure servers until your home connection is restored.

Can I use HomeLink for streaming services? +

Yes! Since you're using your actual home internet connection, you can access all your streaming services, local content, and geo-restricted materials just as if you were at home. This includes Netflix, banking apps, and local news services.
